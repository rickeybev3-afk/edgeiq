1. Purpose of This Declaration

This document establishes, as of the date above, that the following methodologies, systems, and data structures constitute trade secrets of EdgeIQ, as defined under the Defend Trade Secrets Act of 2016 (18 U.S.C. § 1836) and applicable state law. The owner has taken, and will continue to take, reasonable steps to maintain the secrecy of this information.

2. Description of Trade Secrets

The following elements of the EdgeIQ system are designated as proprietary trade secrets:

2a. Total Confidence Score (TCS) Engine

The proprietary multi-factor scoring formula that assigns a confidence rating (0–100) to each trade setup. This includes the specific weighting of input variables, the mathematical relationship between variables, threshold definitions, and the logic by which scores are calculated, normalized, and filtered.

2b. Brain Weight System

The dynamic weight coefficients stored in brain_weights.json and the recalibration algorithm that updates these weights based on historical win rate data. This includes the specific structure-to-weight mappings, the blending formula between bot-verified outcomes and manually logged journal data, and the recalibration cadence and threshold logic.

2c. Day Structure Classification Engine

The proprietary methodology for classifying intraday price action into named structural categories (including but not limited to Trend Day, Neutral Day, Neutral Extreme, and their subtypes), using Initial Balance (IB) ranges, volume profile data, and buy/sell pressure ratios. This includes the specific logic of classify_day_structure(), compute_buy_sell_pressure(), and compute_structure_probabilities().

2d. Cognitive Delta Log Methodology

The system for tracking, logging, and analyzing deviations between system-generated trade recommendations and the user's actual trading decisions. This includes the behavioral scoring model, the deviance analysis framework, and the planned cognitive profiling application of this data.

2e. Training Data & Verified Outcome Dataset

The accumulated dataset of paper trades, verified outcomes, structure classifications, win/loss records, and ranking accuracy data stored in the EdgeIQ Supabase database under user ID a5e1fcab-8369-42c4-8550-a8a19734510c. This dataset constitutes proprietary training data with commercial value.

2f. Recalibration & Meta-Brain Architecture

The planned architecture for routing signals dynamically across multiple verified user profiles based on live market conditions — the "meta-brain" system concept, including the marketplace model for brain licensing, revenue share structure, and the method of aggregating and weighting individual user performance data into institutional-grade signal output.

3. Steps Taken to Maintain Secrecy

  • Core algorithmic logic is not published in any public repository
  • Brain weight coefficients and TCS formula are stored in private, access-controlled infrastructure
  • Any party accessing the system or its methodology is required to execute a Non-Disclosure Agreement (see Part II) prior to access
  • Internal documents referencing the methodology are marked Proprietary & Confidential
  • This declaration is password-protected and stored as a dated, timestamped record

4. Commercial Value

The above trade secrets derive independent commercial value from not being generally known or readily ascertainable. The TCS engine and brain weight system, in particular, represent the core competitive differentiator of the EdgeIQ product and form the basis of planned subscription tiers ranging from $49/month to $15,000/month for institutional access.
