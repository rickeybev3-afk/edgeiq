🏗️ Architecture Critique
The app.py Bloat: 25,000 lines in a single Streamlit file is a massive technical debt. Streamlit reruns the entire script on every interaction; even with caching, a file that size will eventually cause UI latency or "script heartbeat" timeouts.

Recommendation: Decouple the "Filter Funnel" and "Trade Log" into a components/ directory. It’ll make debugging the "Kill Switch" significantly safer.

Idempotency Guards: Using /tmp/ lock files for Telegram alerts is a smart, low-latency way to handle container/server restarts without spamming your phone at 10:47 AM ET.

Database Hygiene: Auto-migrations via backend.py are solid, but the manual SQL requirement for Materialized Views is a potential failure point. If the "Ladder" tab depends on these, ensure your nightly bot validates their existence, or you'll be flying blind on PnL stats during the live transition.

📈 Strategy & Risk Analysis
The 80.8% Win Rate: This is exceptional for small-cap IB breakouts. However, paper trading often ignores the "liquidity gap"—in live small-cap setups, getting filled on a 1,500-share bracket order without moving the price (or getting slippage on the stop-loss) is the real test.

PDT & Concurrent Caps: Hard-coding the _check_pdt_guard at the API level is the right move. With a 7k starting equity in Phase 2, you are well below the 25k PDT floor, so that 3-trade-per-5-day limit is your tightest bottleneck.

2.14% Account Risk: This is a professional-grade risk parameter. It keeps you in the game even during a "max pain" streak.
